**Styrofoam** is a brand of closed-cell [[extruded]] polystyrene foam (XPS), manufactured to provide continuous [building insulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Building_insulation "Building insulation") board used in walls, roofs, and foundations as [thermal insulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thermal_insulation "Thermal insulation") and as a water barrier.
