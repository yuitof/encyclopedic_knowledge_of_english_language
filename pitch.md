A [YouTube](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTube "YouTube") sketch comedian, Barker uploaded his short horror film _The Chair_ to his channel in 2023, which led to an offer from producer James Harris to adapt it into a feature film. Barker took the opportunity to **pitch** _Obsession_, a different film idea partly inspired by the first segment of _[The Simpsons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Simpsons "The Simpsons")_ episode "[Treehouse of Horror II](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treehouse_of_Horror_II "Treehouse of Horror II")", which in turn was primarily a [parody](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parody "Parody") of the short story "[The Monkey's Paw](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Monkey's_Paw)" by [W. W. Jacobs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W._W._Jacobs "W. W. Jacobs").
